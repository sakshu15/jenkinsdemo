Smt. Kashibai Navale Collage of Engineering Pune

     Established in 2001, Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering remains to be a centre for top-class technical education in Pune, Maharashtra. The college offers undergraduate, postgraduate and Ph.D programmes in the field of engineering and management. The college has been accredited with A Grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).

  • undergraduate engineering programme is offered in Electronics & Telecommunication, Computer Science, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering.
  • Spread over an area of 18,532 sq. m, the college maintains a state-of-the-art campus.
  • The library of the college boasts of 18,532 volumes. Further, the college comprises advanced labs, modern classrooms and 696 computer terminals offering a comprehensive learning environment.
  • The fee for the first year of the Bachelors of Engineering programme at Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering is INR 1,02,152.
  • The fee for the first year of the Masters of Engineering (ME) programme at the college is INR 97,227.
  • The fee for the first year of the MBA programme at Smt. Kashibai Navale College of Engineering is INR 1,19,927.
